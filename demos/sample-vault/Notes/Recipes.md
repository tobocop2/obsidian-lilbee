# Recipes

## Sourdough loaf

500g bread flour, 350g water, 100g active starter, 10g salt.
Mix, autolyse 30 min, fold every 30 min for 2.5 hrs, shape, retard
overnight, bake at 480F covered for 20 min then 425F uncovered for 25.

## Chicken stock

Roasted carcass + onion + carrot + celery + bay + peppercorns. Cover
with cold water, simmer 4 hrs, strain.
