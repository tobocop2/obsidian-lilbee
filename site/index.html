what it does

encyclopedia of your vault

Index every note, PDF, ebook, and code file. Every answer clicks back to the source line. A private Encarta you build from your own vault.

verify at the source

Every citation in chat or wiki opens a Source Preview modal scrolled to the exact passage, with the cited lines highlighted. Verification is one click — useful anywhere a confident answer needs proof.

auto-generated wiki

Concept and entity pages compounded across sources, citation-verified, with drafts queued for review. Renders in Obsidian's graph view via [[wiki links]].

scans & OCR

Scanned PDFs and photographed pages go through Tesseract or a local vision GGUF, landing as searchable markdown with layout preserved.
